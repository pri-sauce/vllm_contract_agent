## Executive Summary

This Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) review highlights significant risks and areas for improvement in the contract between SIRENA AI SYSTEMS PRIVATE LIMITED (Disclosing Party) and an undisclosed Receiving Party. A total of 6 clauses were reviewed, with 2 identified as High Risk Issues, 3 as Medium Risk Issues, and 1 clause requiring a NEW CLAUSE.

The top High Risk Issue is the lack of specific notice period for return or destruction of confidential information, which could lead to significant financial and reputational risks if not addressed. Another critical High Risk Issue is the perpetually binding confidentiality obligation, which would impose an indefinite obligation on both parties without clear termination conditions. These clauses require immediate attention to mitigate potential liabilities.

The Medium Risk Issues include perpetual confidentiality obligations in two separate clauses, as well as a clause that could lead to an indefinite obligation to maintain confidentiality. While these issues are not as severe as the High Risk Issues, they still warrant careful review and negotiation to ensure fair and balanced terms. The NEW CLAUSE for the notice period clause is recommended to address this issue.

Based on the review, we strongly advise negotiating key provisions before signing the contract. Specifically, addressing the lack of specific notice period and perpetually binding confidentiality obligations are critical priorities. While some clauses may be acceptable as-is, the identified risks necessitate a cautious approach to ensure both parties' interests are protected.
