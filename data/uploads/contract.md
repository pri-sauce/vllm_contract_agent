### 3. SCOPE OF SERVICES

#### 3.1 Description of Services
The Service Provider shall provide the following IT support services to the Client (the **"Services"**):

3.1.1 **Helpdesk Support**: Provision of first-level and second-level technical support to the Client’s employees via telephone, email, and remote access tools, including troubleshooting hardware and software issues, password resets, and user account management.

3.1.2 **System Maintenance**: Regular maintenance of the Client’s IT systems, including but not limited to servers, workstations, network devices, and peripheral equipment, to ensure optimal performance and security.

3.1.3 **Software Management**: Installation, configuration, updating, and patching of operating systems, applications, and security software on the Client’s IT systems.

3.1.4 **Network Management**: Monitoring, management, and maintenance of the Client’s local area network (LAN), wide area network (WAN), and internet connectivity, including firewall configuration and virtual private network (VPN) management.

3.1.5 **Data Backup and Recovery**: Implementation and management of data backup procedures, including regular testing of backup integrity and provision of data recovery services in the event of data loss.

3.1.6 **Security Services**: Implementation and management of security measures, including antivirus and anti-malware protection, intrusion detection and prevention, and regular security audits.

3.1.7 **Advisory Services**: Provision of strategic advice and recommendations regarding the Client’s IT infrastructure, including hardware and software upgrades, cybersecurity best practices, and compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

3.1.8 **On-Site Support**: Provision of on-site support at the Client’s premises as reasonably required, including hardware installation, network cabling, and emergency response.

3.1.9 The Services shall be provided in accordance with the Service Levels set forth in Section 3.2 and any additional specifications or requirements agreed upon in writing by the Parties from time to time.

#### 3.2 Service Levels
The Service Provider shall perform the Services in accordance with the following service levels (the **"Service Levels"**):

3.2.1 **Response Time**:
   - **Priority 1 (Critical)**: The Service Provider shall acknowledge and begin working on the issue within **one (1) hour** of receipt of the Client’s request.
   - **Priority 2 (High)**: The Service Provider shall acknowledge and begin working on the issue within **four (4) hours** of receipt of the Client’s request.
   - **Priority 3 (Medium)**: The Service Provider shall acknowledge and begin working on the issue within **one (1) Business Day** of receipt of the Client’s request.
   - **Priority 4 (Low)**: The Service Provider shall acknowledge and begin working on the issue within **three (3) Business Days** of receipt of the Client’s request.

3.2.2 **Resolution Time**:
   - **Priority 1 (Critical)**: The Service Provider shall use commercially reasonable efforts to resolve the issue within **four (4) hours** of acknowledgment.
   - **Priority 2 (High)**: The Service Provider shall use commercially reasonable efforts to resolve the issue within **one (1) Business Day** of acknowledgment.
   - **Priority 3 (Medium)**: The Service Provider shall use commercially reasonable efforts to resolve the issue within **three (3) Business Days** of acknowledgment.
   - **Priority 4 (Low)**: The Service Provider shall use commercially reasonable efforts to resolve the issue within **five (5) Business Days** of acknowledgment.
